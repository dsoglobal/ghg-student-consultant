Student Consultant — Web Design

October - December 2016 | Stanford, CA

About the Client

GirlsHelpingGirls is a nonprofit organization that is dedicated to raising the status of women through education. They accomplish this through a three-pronged holistic approach:

  • Build schools to support a healthy and stimulating learning environment.
  • Provide teachers with the proper training and resources so they can best support their students.
  • Create scholarships for students to go to school.

GHG has been able to make this holistic approach a reality by building two schools in Jamaica, providing teacher training in Afghanistan, and by creating scholarships for needy girls in South Africa, Jamaica, and Kenya.

About the Project

GirlsHelpingGirls has partnered with Development Solutions Organization (DSO), a volunteer-driven nonprofit consultancy, to redesign the GHG website. The primary goals of the website update are to:

  • Convey a more modern, professional yet philanthropic brand image to attract higher value donations
  • Better convey the mission, story, and impact of the organization
  • Allow donors to understand the impact of their specific donations
  • Make the website SEO-optimized and mobile-friendly

The project will last from October to December 2016, culminating in a final presentation to key internal and external stakeholders.

Your Role

As a DSO consultant, you will be part of a small, highly collaborative team of student volunteers from Stanford and professional volunteers from the Bay Area's top companies.

Your responsibilities will include:

  • Designing and coding pages for GHG's new website
  • Conducting user testing to validate your designs (show early, show often)
  • Reviewing and critiquing your teammates' work
  • Regularly presenting your work to Susanna, founder of GHG, via Google Hangouts

Your impact potential:

  • Empower GHG to create more opportunities for girls to go to school
  • Create work that will be highly visible within the education and nonprofit communities
  • Apply the technical skills you've learned in class to make a global impact

Requirements

  • You're pursuing a career in software engineering or product design
  • You've built things with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • You're familiar with the basic principles of responsive web design
  • You're a self-starter who can work both independently and collaboratively
  • You're passionate about women's education and international development
  • You can contribute 3-5 volunteer hours per week throughout Fall Quarter

About DSO

Development Solutions Organization is a volunteer-driven consultancy that partners with nonprofits and social enterprises to address some of world's biggest challenges in international development. Our team is comprised of professional volunteers from top companies (McKinsey, Box, Uber, Microsoft) and student volunteers from prestigious universities (Stanford, Carnegie Mellon, Georgetown). Notable past partners include Kiva, Ashoka, Samasource, and Made by Survivors.

Joining DSO grants lifetime access to our projects, partners, and — most importantly — our network of talented people who are passionate about international development.
